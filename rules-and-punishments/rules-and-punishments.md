30\. This includes, but is not limited to:
* directing other players to affiliate links 游릭
* joining the server expressly to direct players to a different server or link 游
* in contexts uncalled-for, linking players to a non-FroobWorld related Discord server. 游

31\. For the avoidance of doubt, the following examples should not be considered advertising:
